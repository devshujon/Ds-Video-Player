# DS Video Player — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 2026  
**Developer:** Dev Shujon

## Overview

DS Video Player is a local media player. Your videos, audio, and photos stay on your device. We do not upload your media library to our servers.

## Data we access

| Data | Purpose | Leaves device? |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Videos, audio, photos on device | Playback and library browsing | No |
| Playback history & favorites | Resume, recommendations, Continue Watching | No (stored locally in SQLite) |
| Vault PIN & encrypted files | Private vault feature | No (stored locally) |
| Purchase status | Premium features via Google Play | Google Play only |
| Ad identifiers (free tier) | Banner/rewarded ads via Google AdMob | AdMob only |

## Permissions

- **Media (video/audio/photos):** Scan and play your local library.
- **Notifications:** Background playback controls.
- **Internet:** Stream URLs, ads (free tier), in-app purchases.
- **Biometric:** Optional vault unlock.

You may deny permissions; the app degrades gracefully (empty library, no background controls).

## Third-party services

- **Google AdMob** — ads on the free tier ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Google Play Billing** — premium purchases ([Google Play Terms](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/))

## Data retention

All library and playback data is stored on your device. Uninstalling the app removes local data unless backed up by Android.

## Children's privacy

DS Video Player is not directed at children under 13.
