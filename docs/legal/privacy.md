# Privacy Policy — DS Video Player

**Effective:** July 2026 · **Developer:** Dev Shujon

DS Video Player plays media stored on your device. We do not operate servers that collect your video library.

## Data on your device

Playback history, favorites, playlists, and vault files are stored locally in SQLite and encrypted storage. Uninstalling the app removes this data unless Android backup restores it.

## Permissions

Media permissions are used only to scan and play your files. Internet is used for optional streaming, in-app purchases, and ads on the free tier.
